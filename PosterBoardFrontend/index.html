What is This Site?

Help raise awareness on any topic you feel needs to be put out there. Using this website, anyone can anonymously share their feelings, opinions, or thoughts on important social, economic, or ethical issues happening around the globe. Many of us are to shy to speak out publicly, and share what might be a controversial opinion. Use this site to share with others. Have fun!
In order to use this site, click the "Create Post" button. There, you will see two text boxes. Text goes into top textbox, while the bottom one holds a link to an image. Images are optional. After you have completed your post, click the "Publish" button, and you're set! You should see your post on the website. All posts delete at midnight.
